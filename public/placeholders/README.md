## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Dimensions**: 512x512 pixels (square)
- **Background**: Transparent or pure black (#000000)
- **Composition**: Centered creature/egg
- **Style**: Dark fantasy horror, creepy cute aesthetic
- **File Size**: Recommended < 200KB per image

Placeholder images are loaded first while AI-generated art is being created. They provide instant visual feedback and a consistent experience even when AI services are unavailable.
